Dawn & Dusk
is a single player 2D survival game that relies on finding food and fighting off enemies in order to survive. A random character will be created and spawned into a random environment when the game starts. Each character will have different skills and each environment will present the player with different challenge. The player have to look out for items all around the island and utilize them to have a better chance to survive.

About Dawn & Dusk
After the shipwrecked, the journey starts with the protagonist being on an abandoned island. The protagonist has to survive and try to find a way to get off the island. The player has to find food and fight off monsters that appeared to survive. Upon exploring the island, the player realises that the island has certain secrets and has to slowly uncover them.
